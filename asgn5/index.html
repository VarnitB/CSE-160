Pirate Treasure Cove

A Three.js pirate island scene with textured objects, a loaded GLB ship, a pirate captain model, animated ocean motion, lights, a skybox, and mouse controls.

Controls

Orbit View:

Left click + drag: rotate camera

Scroll: zoom

Right click + drag: pan

Pirate's View:

Move mouse or drag to look around from the pirate captain's position.

Rubric Checklist

  • 20+ primary shapes
  • 3+ shape types: cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones, torus
  • Textured primary shapes
  • Custom textured GLB ship model
  • Custom GLB pirate captain model
  • 3+ light sources
  • Perspective camera
  • OrbitControls mouse navigation
  • Textured skybox background
  • Animated objects
  • Wow feature: Pirate's View camera mode
Wow Feature: Click Pirate's View to see the island from the pirate captain's point of view.
