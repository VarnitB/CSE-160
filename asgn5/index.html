Pirate Treasure Cove

A Three.js island scene with textured objects, a loaded GLB pirate ship, animated treasure, ocean motion, skybox, lights, and mouse controls.

Controls

Left click + drag: rotate camera

Scroll: zoom

Right click + drag: pan

Click the glowing treasure for the wow feature.
