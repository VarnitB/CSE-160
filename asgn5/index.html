Pirate Treasure Cove

A Three.js pirate island scene with textured objects, a loaded GLB ship, a walking pirate captain, animated ocean motion, lights, a skybox, and mouse controls.

Light Controls

Controls

Orbit View:

Left click + drag: rotate camera

Scroll: zoom

Right click + drag: pan

Pirate's View:

The camera follows the walking pirate captain. Move your mouse slightly to look left, right, up, and down.

Rubric Checklist

  • 20+ primary shapes
  • 3+ shape types: cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones, torus
  • Textured primary shapes
  • Custom textured GLB ship model
  • Custom GLB pirate captain model
  • 3+ light sources: AmbientLight, HemisphereLight, DirectionalLight, PointLight, and SpotLight
  • Interactive light toggles
  • Perspective camera
  • OrbitControls mouse navigation
  • Textured skybox background
  • Animated objects
  • Wow feature: walking pirate POV camera mode
